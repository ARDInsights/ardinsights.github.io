The Verifiability Gradient

Why human oversight breaks when AI output stops being checkable.

Most oversight assumes that someone can check the work. However, a growing volume of AI output can't be checked, or costs as much to check as to redo. This paper sorts AI output into 4 groups by how checkable it is and then says what control belongs to each. Written for CROs and model-risk leaders, with an executive summary that stands on its own.
